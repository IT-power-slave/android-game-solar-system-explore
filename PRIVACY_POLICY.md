# Privacy Policy for Solar System Explorers

**Last Updated: June 18, 2026**

Solar System Explorers ("we", "our", or "the App") is committed to protecting the privacy of our users, especially children. This Privacy Policy explains our data practices.

## 1. Data Collection
We do **not** collect any Personal Identifiable Information (PII) such as names, email addresses, or phone numbers. 

The App stores the following data **locally on your device**:
*   Game points (pts)
*   Earned badges
*   Visited planets
*   Quiz progress

This data is used solely to provide game features and is not transmitted to any external servers.

## 2. Third-Party Services
The App does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.

## 3. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)
The App is designed for educational purposes and is suitable for children. Because we do not collect any personal information, we are compliant with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.
